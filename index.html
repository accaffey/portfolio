Hello,

I am fascinated with outer space, inspired by people who help others and passionate about creating accepting and inclusive spaces for everyone.

Unfortunately, I did not pursue my childhood dream job of becoming an astronaut so, until civilians can vacation to the moon, I’ll be staring at the stars from Earth. I turned to another passion – inspiring people. I love finding unique ways to showcase stories of athletes, organizations and community members stepping up to help and inspire their communities.

I graduated from Syracuse University with a degree in Broadcast and Digital Journalism and from the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications and Citizenship and Civic Engagement at the Maxwell School of Citizenship and Public Affairs. I moved back to Houston where I worked as a job coach for high school students with disabilities before starting my career with the Houston Texans.
