The Daily Orange

The Daily Orange is a student-run, independent newspaper and was one of my favorite places at Syracuse University. When I started working at the paper, the publication averaged three videos per semester. With very little to work with, I designed a video style and brand for The Daily Orange. As a video editor, I built and led a team of videographers to cover events and people on and around campus. At the end of my career, we averaged three videos per week.

Remembrance Week

Thirty-five Syracuse University students were aboard Pan Am Flight 103 when it went down over Lockerbie, Scotland in 1988. Every year, Syracuse University takes a week to honor their lives. I reached out to the local stations for news coverage of the event, shot the footage on campus, and helped edit this video to preview Remembrance Week 2017.

Inauguration

In January 2017, I traveled to Washington D.C. to cover the inauguration, protests, and the Women’s Day March. As a team, we published four videos — I shot all the Washington D.C. footage, edited this video and oversaw production for the others.

Basketball Graphic

The sports section at the paper produces a ‘Basketball Guide’ at the beginning of each season and I animated short graphic videos to supplement the online presence.
