Inclusive Employment

I researched job placement programs that universities have in place for students with intellectual and developmental disabilities. At Syracuse University, placing students with IDD after they’ve graduated has been difficult. This finding is consistent with national employment rates for people with disabilities. Despite employment incentives and training programs, the employment rates for people with and without disabilities are drastically different.

I developed an Employment Workshop for students with Intellectual and Developmental Disabilities at Syracuse University in the InclusiveU program. The students created LinkedIn profiles, took proffesional headshots, built resumes, and recorded video cover letters.
